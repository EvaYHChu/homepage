MRI research

From MRI hardware to data analysis, my recent studies are here in this section.

I built and designed the MRI RF coils. A 32-channel head coil and its images were shown. Also, 24-channel field probes were constructed and were applied to characterize the MRI gradient system. With the information, we can correct the deviation caused by the gradient coil imperfection.

Works

A 32-channel head coil array with circularly symmetric geometry (CS array)

This coil is designed for acquiring highly accelerated parallel human brain imaging. The maximal SNR increased 120% and 62% compared to the commercial soccer-ball coil array at the periphery and the center of the FOV on a transverse plane, respectively.

24-channel field probes for dynamical magnetic field monitoring

We measured and decouple this signal coupling between field probes and applied it to monitor the dynamic magnetic field during diffusion-sensitive EPI measurements. Erroneously estimated magnetic field oscillation under constant gradient field was greatly reduced after applying the proposed probe decoupling method. The dynamic magnetic map with up to the 4th-order spatial polynomials was measured and applied to improve the image reconstruction. Decoupled probes also led to reduced image artifact and improved image registration than from images using coupled probe signals.
